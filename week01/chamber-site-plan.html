Site Name

The site will be named Mini-Cassia Chamber of Commerce. This name makes clear the counties covered by the chamber of commerce.

Site Purpose

The primary goals of this site are to encourage the community to participate with the chamber of commerce, keep users up to date with current events, and help users understand what the chamber of commerce does.

Scenarios

  • How can I communicate with the chamber of commerce?
  • What can I do to support local buisnesses?
  • What is there to see in the area?

Color Schema

  • Light Green: to be used in the header and footer as backround.
  • Dark Green: to be used as borders, and in other accents
  • Smoky black: to be used for text in the header and footer.

Typography

This site will use the Libre Baskerville font family.
